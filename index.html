  • About IEEE

    IEEE is a prestigious professional association with more than 400,000 technology and engineering professionals with an alike desire to learn and innovate the world every second. By joining IEEE you will get access to world-class resources and opportunities to showcase your skills.

    The strong professional network will not only boost your community reach but will also mentor you for becoming a next-generation engineer or technologist. IEEE members get extra privileges and information about events and activities by signing up with IEEE Collabratec, an immense network of technology-focused professionals, and many more benefits.

    IEEE members have access to: Network with other technology professionals Establish a professional profile highlighting your accomplishments Join and participate in discussions on various technical interests Create a group to share and collaborate on projects which will help in improving your portfolio Discover IEEE events and activities throughout the world and stay updated with the advancing world Joining IEEE will not only give you access to brilliant resources and information, being an active member will also help you in placements. IEEE-issued certificates have a great impact on resumes and will certainly set you apart during your placements. Fastrack your career by joining IEEE !!
